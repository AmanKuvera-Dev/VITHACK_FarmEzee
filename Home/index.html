About Us

An organization that develops, upgrades and manages a blockchain based crypto currency newtwork that is completly Farmer-centric. The main motto is to introduce the farmers to a digitized currency and decide the cost of their goods by themseves like manufacturers do whilst eliminating the middleman costs for sales by carring it out through our user-friendly web interface and reducing the trnasportation costs.
