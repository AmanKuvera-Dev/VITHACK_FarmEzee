About Us

An organization that develops, upgrades and manages a blockchain based crypto currency newtwork that is completly Farmer-centric. The main motto is to introduce the farmers to a digitized currency and decide the cost of their goods by themseves like manufacturers do whilst eliminating the middleman costs for sales by carring it out through our user-friendly web interface and reducing the trnasportation costs.

Features

FarmEzee is an application of blockchain technology with its integration to the web frameworks. FarmEzee has multiple features that stand-out and reflect their novelties while supporting the Farmers with their never ending problems of market prices and middleman fradulance,

Farmico Currency

Farmico is the crypto Currency that runs the blockchain system. This currency was introduced to let the farmer expanded his market overseas with a generalized currency. It helps the farmer to redeem it to local currency through farmEzee banks and can be kept track of it the farmers portal.

Web-based Market

A fully functional web based market that provides facilities of buy and sell of items and redeem the Farmico coins into local currency. It also provides anlysis abt the transactions and keeps track of history of transactions.

Logistics

FarmEzee provides Logistics for the orders placed by the consumers so that the cost of transportation can be minimized by avoiding third party transportation services. Both the consumer and the farmers are provided with all the transporters details through the FarmEzee Web portal.

How Does It Work?

Blockchain technology

Blockchain.com (formerly Blockchain.info) is a Bitcoin block explorer service, as well as a cryptocurrency wallet and a cryptocurrency exchange supporting Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, and Ethereum. They also provide Bitcoin data charts, stats, and market information.
Blockchain.com was launched in August 2011. It was founded by Ben Reeves who was part of the original founding team at Coinbase. The two had a differing opinion on the future of Coinbase, so Reeves left Coinbase to start Blockchain.com. The service provides data on recent transactions, mined blocks in the bitcoin blockchain, charts on the bitcoin economy, and statistics and resources for developers.
